lasagna

“Description”

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is perfect for a comforting dinner. This recipe is a delicious blend of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and tender pasta layers. It's a great dish for feeding a crowd, for special occasions or for meal prepping. The recipe is easy to follow and can be adjusted to suit your taste. You can use ground beef or sausage, or even make it a vegetarian version by substituting meat with vegetables. The best part is that it can be made ahead and reheated, making it a perfect dinner option for busy weeknights. This recipe is sure to become a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg (optional)
  • parsley or basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until they are al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or sausage over medium heat. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  5. Stir in tomatoes or tomato sauce, salt, pepper, basil, and oregano. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, and parmesan cheese together with an egg (if desired).
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used up, making sure to finish with a layer of meat sauce and cheese on top.
  11. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is golden brown and bubbly.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving.
  14. Garnish with parsley or basil (if desired) and enjoy!
