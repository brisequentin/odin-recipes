cookies

“Description”

These cookies are the perfect treat for any occasion. They are easy to make and have a delicious, classic taste that everyone will love. The recipe is simple and requires only a few basic ingredients that you probably already have in your pantry. You can make them plain or add chocolate chips, nuts, or any other mix-ins to make them extra special. They are perfect for a snack, a dessert or for gift giving. These cookies are sure to become a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter or Margarine
  • Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius).
  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  3. In a separate large mixing bowl, cream together butter or margarine and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, to the butter mixture, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually stir in the flour mixture to the butter mixture.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract and chocolate chips (if desired)
  7. Roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
