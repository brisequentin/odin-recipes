pancakes

“Description”

These pancakes are a classic breakfast staple that can be enjoyed in so many ways. With a fluffy texture and a perfect balance of sweetness, they're sure to please everyone at the table. The recipe is simple and easy to follow, making it perfect for a weekend brunch or a quick and easy week day breakfast. The ingredients are common pantry staples and can be easily adjusted to suit your taste. Add some chocolate chips, blueberries, or bananas to make it more interesting. These pancakes are sure to become a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter or oil
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate small mixing bowl, beat the eggs.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the flour mixture and add milk, melted butter or oil, and vanilla extract (if desired
  4. Mix until smooth, but a few small lumps are okay.
  5. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  6. Use a ladle or measuring cup to pour batter onto the skillet or griddle.
  7. Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on one side or until small bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side.
